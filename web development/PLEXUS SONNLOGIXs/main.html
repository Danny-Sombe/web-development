The Plexus SONNLogix Sensor is an advanced smart monitoring device designed to enhance data accuracy, efficiency, and reliability across industrial and environmental applications.

Built with high-precision sensing technology, it continuously captures real-time data such as dates, times, and movement, ensuring seamless integration into existing systems.

Equipped with intelligent connectivity features, the Plexus SONNLogix Sensor utilizes modern communication protocols such as Wi-Fi, LoRa, or Bluetooth to transmit data securely to the SONNLogix cloud platform. This platform provides users with a centralized dashboard for visualizing trends, generating alerts, and analyzing performance metrics.

Overall, the Plexus SONNLogix Sensor represents the next step in smart sensing technology—offering reliability, scalability, and intelligence for a wide range of applications, from logistics and fleet tracking to environmental monitoring and automation systems.

Arduino ESP32 Sensor:

Arduino ESP32 sensor provides real-time monitoring and data collection capabilities.

Features:

  • Arduino-compatible
  • Real-time data logging
  • High-precision motion detection

  • Multiple sensor support (movement, sound, etc.)

Company Overview

SonnLogix is a Papua New Guinea–based logistics technology company focused on delivering intelligent, cloud-based solutions for supply chain and transport operations. We empower logistics businesses, SMEs, and enterprises to manage their entire operation—from warehousing to delivery—through smart, integrated software. Our goal is to make logistics more efficient, transparent, and data-driven across PNG and the Pacific region.

Mission

To empower businesses with intelligent logistics software that simplifies movement, increases productivity, and enhances real-time visibility across the supply chain.

Vision

To become Papua New Guinea’s leading logistics software provider, connecting every business in the Pacific through digital innovation and smarter logistics management.
